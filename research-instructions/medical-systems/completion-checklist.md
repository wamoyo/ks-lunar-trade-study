## Data Quality Criteria

### For Each System in JSON:
- [ ] Vendor name verified and current
- [ ] Model/product name accurate
- [ ] TRL documented with source URL
- [ ] TRL ≥ 5 (or explicitly noted as exception)
- [ ] At least 3 of 6 key specs documented (mass, power, cost, dimensions, turnaround, heritage)
- [ ] Source URLs functional and relevant
- [ ] Notes explain any limitations or gaps

### Cross-Referencing:
- [ ] Major systems verified across ≥2 independent sources
- [ ] Conflicting information noted and researched
- [ ] Vendor contact information included where available

### TRL Distribution Check:
- [ ] TRL 9 (operational): ≥10 systems documented
- [ ] TRL 8 (qualified): ≥5 systems documented
- [ ] TRL 7 (demonstration): ≥5 systems documented
- [ ] TRL 6 (prototype): ≥5 systems documented
- [ ] TRL 5 (validation): ≥5 systems documented
- [ ] **Total: ≥30 systems** minimum at TRL 5+
