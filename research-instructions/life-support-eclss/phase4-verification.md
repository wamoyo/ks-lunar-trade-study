### Collins Aerospace (Hamilton Sundstrand) - ISS Water Processor Assembly (WPA)
- **Field:** trl
- **Correction:** Confirmed TRL 9 - Launched STS-126 November 2008, operational since November 2008. Wikipedia source tier: caution. CORRECTED: was incorrectly listed as operational since 2000

### Honeywell Aerospace - ISS Carbon Dioxide Removal Assembly (CDRA)
- **Field:** trl
- **Correction:** Confirmed TRL 9 - Launched UF-2 February 2001, operational since February 2001. Two CDRAs installed on ISS. Honeywell vendor site (authoritative). CORRECTED vendor: Honeywell (not Collins)

### NASA MSFC / Precision Combustion Inc. - ISS Carbon Dioxide Reduction Assembly (Sabatier Reactor)
- **Field:** trl
- **Correction:** Confirmed TRL 9 - Delivered April 2010, activated October 2010, operational on ISS. Wikipedia source tier: caution. CORRECTED: was listed as operational April 2010, actually activated October 2010
