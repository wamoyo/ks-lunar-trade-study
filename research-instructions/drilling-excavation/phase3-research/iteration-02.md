## Issues Identified

### TRL Uncertainty
**Problem:** 11/17 systems lack published TRL data
**Impact:** Difficult to confirm TRL 5+ threshold
**Possible Solutions:**
1. Accept "development/testing" as implied TRL 4-6
2. Note TRL uncertainty in Phase 4 verification
3. Focus verification on the 6 systems with known TRL 5+

### Limited Drilling Systems
**Problem:** Only 3 drilling systems found (target was 5)
**Assessment:** May represent actual market reality
**Options:**
1. Accept 3 systems as complete coverage
2. Lower threshold to TRL 4 for drilling only
3. Include Mars-focused systems (RedWater) if adaptable

**Recommendation:** Accept 3 systems. The drilling market appears less developed than excavation. TRIDENT (TRL 6) and PVEx (TRL 5) are the clear leaders.

### Commercial vs Government
**Problem:** Several "commercial" companies only have government contracts
**Examples:** GITAI (JAXA contract), Honeybee (NASA missions), Redwire (NASA challenge)
**Assessment:** This is normal for space industry - commercial companies develop via government contracts then commercialize
**Action:** Mark as "commercial development" vs "purely government"
