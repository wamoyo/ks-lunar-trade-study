### Item 10: RegoLight Consortium - Solar Sintering System
- **TRL confirmed and enhanced:** "5 (near TRL 6)" correct (Source: authoritative tier - liquifer.com, regolight.eu)
  - EU Horizon 2020 project (Nov 2015 - Apr 2018) advanced from TRL 3 to TRL 5 as planned, with near TRL 6 achieved via vacuum chamber demonstration at DLR Cologne (Nov 2017, 1.1 MW/m² solar simulator, ~2 mbar pressure)
- **Technology type verified:** "Additive Layer Manufacturing (ALM) via Concentrated Solar Energy" - three methods evaluated: moving sand bed, moving solar beam, beam concentration into fiber optic
- **Funding verified:** EU Horizon 2020 Grant Agreement No. 686202
- **Material properties corrected:** Added compressive strength data: 2.49 MPa (solar sintering), up to 152 MPa (vacuum furnace sintering of JSC-1A at 1100°C). Solar sintering ~50x weaker than laser melting but requires zero electrical power.
- **Zero electrical power advantage:** Only zero-electrical-power sintering system identified. Uses only sunlight, but limited to lunar day operations (~14 Earth days per cycle)
- **Consortium verified:** DLR Cologne (Germany, lead), Space Applications Services (Belgium), LIQUIFER Systems Group (Austria), COMEX (France), Bollinger Grohmann Schneider (Austria)
- **All fields upgraded:** 13 fields moved from verified:false to authoritative/credible/unverified tiers with detailed notes
